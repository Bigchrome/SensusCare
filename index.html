About Sensus Care

Sensus Care is a remote patient monitoring platform for patients with mood disorders.
We feel that psychiatrists and patients alike can benefit by more frequent ‘automated’ communication. We aim to achieve this via delivery of daily questionnaires to patients (through smartphones or the web) which aim to assess the patients’ mood. Our software attempts to determine whether the patient is stable and if not, deliver more questions to better understand the phase the patient is in.

What we believe is the real innovation of our platform, is its capability to automatically recognise when a patient might need help and alert their doctor. Furthermore, it can make it easier to monitor several important variables, such as a patient’s progress, treatment compliance, medication and treatment history, and history of episodes and relapses.

The Solution

We are building a platform for psychiatrists to remotely monitor patients with mood disorders. The aim is to have daily questionnaires present in an app on patients’ smartphones, which will be used to assess the patient’s mental state. This critical info will be accessible by doctors on a daily basis, while states deemed critical will trigger alerts, notifying psychiatrists of developments.
