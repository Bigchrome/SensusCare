What is Sensus Care?

Sensus Care is a new concept for remote patient monitoring aiming to support the treatment process of a broad range of mood disorders.
Sensus Care was created by Sensus Technologies, which consists of a team of 6 young tech entrepreneurs from all over Europe.
The team was created through the EU-XCEL program funded by the European Union. EU-XCEL is a European Venture Program
and focused on creating and supporting European tech entrepreneurs to build information and communication technology (ICT) startups.
